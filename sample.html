96

Featured Review: "96"

Director: C. Prem Kumar
Genre: Romance/Drama
Rating: ★★★★★

Synopsis:

"96" is a heartwarming tale of love, longing, and nostalgia...

Storyline:

Set in the backdrop of a school reunion, "96" follows the journey of Ram and Janu, former classmates who haven't seen each other in over two decades. As they reminisce about their past and the unfulfilled promises of their youth, they rediscover the depth of their connection and the power of first love. Through a series of flashbacks, the audience is transported back to the days of innocence and passion, as Ram and Janu confront their regrets and confront the reality of what could have been.

Review:

C. Prem Kumar's "96" is a masterpiece that delicately captures the essence of romance and the bittersweet nature of memories...

Final Verdict:

An emotional rollercoaster that will leave you reminiscing about your own past loves and moments.
