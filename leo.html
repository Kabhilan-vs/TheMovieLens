Leo

Featured Review: "Leo"

Director:Lokesh Kanagaraj
Genre: Action/Thriller
Rating: ★★★☆☆

Synopsis:

Things start to take an awry turn for a mild-mannered cafe owner, who gets caught in the crosshairs of a drug cartel.

Storyline:

Parthiban is a mild-mannered cafe owner in Kashmir, who fends off a gang of murderous thugs and gains attention from a drug cartel claiming he was once a part of them.

Review:

Leo has one of the best action sequences that I have seen in Indian movies in the recent times. The 2 fights in the first half are really well choreographed especially the action scene at the market. Anirudh slowly elevating background score does help the cause as well.
